About Us

About DecodeMTL

We are currently running our courses out of La Commune, an awesome co-working space in the heart of Montreal's old port. Our goal is to bring coding literacy to as many people as possible. We believe that with the right environment, best teachers, and partnerships with the right companies, we can fuel local, national, and international organizations with a new wave of talented developers.
