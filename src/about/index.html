About Us

About DecodeMTL

We are currently running our courses out of WeWork Place Ville Marie, an awesome co-working space in the heart of Montreal. Our goal is to bring coding literacy to as many people as possible. We believe that with the right environment, best teachers, and partnerships with the right companies, we can fuel local, national, and international organizations with a new wave of talented developers.

Meet the team

We're a passionate bunch of technological educators from Montreal, Quebec. Our number one goal is to ensure that you achieve great things after graduating from DecodeMTL.
