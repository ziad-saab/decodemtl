  • Vendredi, 3 Juin @ 15h30

    Demo Day

    Looking for smart, hungry web developers to join your team? Come meet the graduates from our Web Development Immersive program! Over an 8-week program taught by industry experts and thought leaders, our graduates have covered a wide range of technologies - including HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Node, React), SQL, along with other languages and frameworks!

    This is a public event and we encourage anyone interested in hiring our students, working with us, or attending our courses to come and join! The event is setup as follows:

    • 3:30PM - 4:00PM - Registration
    • 4:00PM - 5:30PM - Student Demos
    • 5:30PM - 6:30PM - Closing Comments & Networking

    DecodeMTL does not collect a commission for hiring our graduates and never will.

    Can’t make it? Email kevin@decodemtl.com to find out how you can still get in touch with our graduates.

    Session d'information développement web
